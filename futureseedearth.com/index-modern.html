Our Initiatives

We work across multiple sectors to create sustainable change and environmental impact

Ecosystem Restoration

We conduct tree plantation drives throughout the year. We have pledged to plant 500,000 saplings by the year 2030.

Awareness Programs

FSEF conducts awareness programs at various locations in collaboration with other organizations to educate as many people as possible about environmental health.

Renewable Energy

We work towards promoting Renewable Sources of Energy and save our mother planet from climate change, global warming and pollution.

Women's Health (BHAVATI)

Our BHAVATI initiative focuses on menstrual hygiene awareness, providing education and sanitary products to women in rural and urban areas.

Community Development (HOSLA)

HOSLA focuses on community empowerment through education, skill development, and sustainable livelihood programs.

Research & Education

We conduct research on environmental challenges and provide educational programs to promote sustainable practices.
