FUTURESEED EARTH FOUNDATION

A Seed For Sustainable Development

With a goal of preserving the Environment for Future Generations, the Futureseed Earth Foundation was founded in August 2019. We think it is our duty to take action and alter the society in which we live. We concentrate on a variety of environmental challenges, like climate change, deforestation, pollution, renewable energy, agriculture and rural development, loss of biodiversity, human health, and women's empowerment, all from the standpoint of research, education, and awareness.
To discover long-term solutions and spread awareness of these concerns, we collaborate with regional businesses, governments, and communities. Our team is made up of passionate and devoted people that are determined to have a positive impact on the environment.

The Futureseed Earth Foundation is made up of enthusiastic young people that value original thinking and unconventional solutions. The Futureseed Earth Foundation actively engages youngsters for a brighter future, and we are grateful to have such creative minds on our side.

Our Vision

"Our vision at Futureseed Earth Foundation is to create a world where the natural environment is protected, preserved, and thriving for current and future generations.

We envision a future where Sustainable Practices are the norm, where Communities and Businesses work together to reduce their impact on the Planet, and where individuals understand the Importance of Preserving the Environment and take action to make a difference.
We see a world where the Air is Clean, the Water is Pure, and the Wildlife is Protected. Where the forests are lush, the Oceans are Teeming with Life, and the Natural Beauty of the Earth is Preserved for all to Enjoy.
We are Committed to Achieving this Vision Through Education, Advocacy, and Action. We believe that by working together, we can create a more Sustainable Future for All and Protect the Planet for Generations to Come."

Our Mission

Mission 1:Plant 10 Lakh Trees by the End of 2025 all over the INDIA.

Mission 2: Awareness and research-based solutions for Air, Water and Plastic Pollution.

Mission 3: Establishment of a Dedicated Research and Training Centre for Environment & Health.

Mission 4:Women and Children (Health, Education) Empowerment.
