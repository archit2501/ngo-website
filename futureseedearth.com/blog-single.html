PLANTATION DRIVE WORLD ENVIRONMENT DAY 2020

  • Abhishek
  • Jun 5, 2020
  • 12 Comments

World Environment Day 2020 was the first world environment day for the organisation and the same was celebrated by planting saplings at various locations by the team and the volunteers involved. Not only individual saplings were planted but on the same day the organisation had adopted 5 primary schools in the district of Bareilly, Uttar Pradesh and pledged to make the premises of these schools green and sustainable. The organisation regularly visits the schools and does the needful accordingly.

VRAKSHAM

“The best time to plant a tree was 20 years ago. The second best time is now.”

Using this as a slogan, we are encouraging tree plantation, which is the need of the hour because of the rapid increase in air pollution and climate change. FSE foundation has been running this plantation drive by encouraging people to celebrate any special day (Birthdaya, Anniversaries etc.) of their lives or their loved ones by planting or dedicating a sapling for them. Through this drive, we have been able to plant more than around 1000 saplings so far and getting a very good feedback.

INDEPENDENCE DAY & KARGIL VIJAY DIWAS 2020

The organisation try to work on field every day and tries its best to not let these special event goes unnoticed. Futureseed Earth Foundation Planted 350 sapling on the occasion of 74th Independence Day 2020 in a Govt. Boys Sr. Sec. School in Sector 3 Dwarka Delhi and 150 saplings of different trees at different locations throughout India, to commemorate our soldiers on Kargil vijay diwas 2020.

GOKAL GARH, REWARI PLANTATION DRIVE

This drive was conducted in association with BABA SITARAM YUVA SHAKTI SAMITI on the occasion of local sport meet for youth and children in Rewari. Team FSEF along with the Baba Sitaram Yuva Shakti Samiti planted 100 sapling at the sport ground and also conducted an awareness about the importance of tress and green cover.

GOKAL GARH, REWARI PLANTATION DRIVE

Futureseed Earth Foundation had conducted this planation awareness program at Rakabganj Gurudwara, New Delhi on 11 April 2021 in association with MAP Studios. The organisation distributed 50 saplings to the participants (cyclist group Turbanators) and other present in the event and also aware them about the importance of tree planation for a sustainable society.
