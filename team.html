Our Team

No matter how smart, talented, driven, or passionate a person is, success depends on ability to build, inspire and work as a team. A successful team is one who can spur with all members to work well and together, towards a common vision / goal.

Rahul Tomar

"Meet Rahul Tomar, the Founder of Futureseed Earth Foundation. Rahul is a dedicated environmentalist with a Postgraduate Degree in Environmental Science.
With a passion for protecting and preserving the natural environment, Rahul has dedicated his life to Advocating for Environmental Issues and Promoting Sustainable Practices.
Rahul has a deep understanding of the Environmental Challenges facing our Planet and is Committed to Finding solutions to Protect the Environment for Future Generations. With his Education and Experience in Environmental Science, Rahul brings a unique perspective and expertise to the work of the NGO.
Rahul is a visionary leader and works tirelessly to raise awareness, promote sustainable practices, and advocate for policy change. Under Rahul's leadership, Futureseed Earth Foundation has become a leading voice in the Environmental Movement, working towards a more sustainable future for all."

Upender Singh - Project Coordinator

Upender Singh is a passionate advocate for sustainability and serves as the Project Coordinator at FUTURESEED EARTH FOUNDATION. With a postgraduate degree in Life Sciences, Upender brings a deep understanding of environmental systems and their critical role in human well-being. His expertise drives the planning and execution of our initiatives, from tree plantation drives to community awareness campaigns. Upender’s dedication to combating climate change and promoting sustainable practices ensures that our projects, such as the BHAVTI Menstrual Hygiene Drive and tree-planting efforts targeting 500,000 saplings by 2030, are impactful and well-coordinated. His leadership fosters collaboration with communities, businesses, and volunteers, creating meaningful change for a sustainable future.
