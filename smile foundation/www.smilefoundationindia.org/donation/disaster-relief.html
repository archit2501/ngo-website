Donate to Support Flood Affected Children & Families

Floods and landslides have struck several parts of India, impacting over 30 lakh people and leaving thousands of families across India in desperate need of help. Homes have been destroyed, communities submerged, lives and livelihoods lost. The situation is dire, and immediate relief is crucial.

Smile Foundation’s disaster response team is on the ground, reaching out to the worst affected children and families to meet their urgent needs. Your generous contribution can help us deliver:

Food Kits: Essential items to sustain a family of five for one month (Rice, dal, salt, oil, sugar, chilli powder, turmeric powder, biscuits, milk powder, electral powder)

Hygiene Kits: Basic hygiene needs to prevent the outbreak of diseases (Sanitary napkins, soaps, chlorine tablets, candles, mosquito repellent, bleaching powder)

Health Camps: Mobile health camps to provide immediate medical care

Your support will help us provide life-saving relief and bring hope to those who have lost so much. Every donation, no matter the size, makes a difference.
