Ditto Insurance Champions Every Child in School Initiative with Smile Foundation

Smile Foundation and Ditto Insurance have collaborated to advance the Every Child in School education initiative, dedicated to providing uninterrupted learning opportunities for children from underserved communities across India. Through this collaboration, Ditto Insurance is supporting a holistic education model that integrates foundational literacy and numeracy (FLN), digital learning tools, and age-appropriate curriculum to ensure well-rounded development. In addition to academic learning, the programme also emphasises behavioural transformation, health awareness, and personality development, enabling children to grow with confidence and dignity.

Founded in 2021 by the team behind Finshots and backed by Zerodha, Ditto Insurance offers clear, unbiased insurance advice via phone and WhatsApp—completely free and spam-free. Renowned for its transparent service and dedicated customer support before and after policy purchase, Ditto has earned a 4.9-star rating and over 12,000 positive reviews on Google. 

This partnership reflects Ditto Insurance’s commitment to social responsibility by empowering young learners to build a brighter, more equitable future
